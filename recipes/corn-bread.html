Corn Bread

Description

This recipe is a delight. The second in my mom's recipe book, this baked good is great as a side in many southern dinners. Corn bread pairs well with chilli, soups, roasts and more.

Ingredients

  • one and a half cups of yellow corn meal
  • one cup of flour
  • a third cup of sugar
  • one heaping tablespoon of baking powder
  • one teaspoon of salt
  • one and one half cups of milk
  • one and one half sticks of butter melted and cooled
  • two eggs beaten

Instructions

  1. Combine wet and dry Ingredients, stir just until moistened
  2. pour into greased pans
  3. bake at 400 degreese till golden
  4. cool completely
