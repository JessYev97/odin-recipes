The Very Best Sandwich Loaf

This is listed in the book as 'Amanda's Bread Recipe'. The recipe yields two loaves. Amanda Paladini from Welcome Homestead is who this is credited to. It looks like a great bread to bake!

Ingredients

  • six and a half to eight and a half cups all purpose flour
  • two and a half teaspoons salt
  • one and a half tablespoons of instant yeast
  • a fourth cup of sugar or honey
  • two and three fourth cups of very warm water
  • a fourth cup of oil

Directions

  1. In the bowl of an electric stand mixer fitted with the dough hook combe THREE cups of the flour, salt, yeast and sugar.
  2. Add the water and oil then mix
  3. Let the batter rest for 10 minutes
  4. Gradually add another 3 to 4 cups of flour until the dough comes together in a cohesive ball
  5. Knead for 2 to 4 minutes
  6. Grease a large bowl transfer dough to bowl, cover, and let rise until doubled
  7. Lightly punch down the dough and divide into two
  8. Grease two bread pans
  9. press each piece of dough into a rectangle, roll it up, press on the seams and pinch final seam together
  10. place dough into prepared pans
  11. cover and let rise until doubled
  12. while dough rises pre heat oven to 350 degrees F
  13. Bake for 30 to 32 minutes until golden brown
  14. turn bread out onto wire rack and brush tops with melted butter
