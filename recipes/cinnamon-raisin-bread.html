Cinnamon Raisin Bread

Description

This recipe is the first in a baking cook book my mom put together for me. This, like many of the other recipes in the book, is hand written. It's a sentimental for this reason. Cinnamon rasin bread will fill up the house with a warm and welcoming aroma. This recipe is a pleasure to make and a pleasure to eat.

The following is a list of the ingredients needed to bake this bread. You will see warm water on the list. This ingredient is essential in activating the yeast which is a living organism. You have to be careful to not overheat the water as this could kill the yeast.

Ingredients

  • one and one half cups of milk
  • one cup of warm water
  • two packets of active dry yeast
  • one cup of raisins
  • three large eggs
  • one and one fourth cups of sugar
  • a half cup of softened margarine
  • one teaspoon of salt
  • eight cups of all purpose flour
  • two tablespoons of milk
  • two tablespoons of cinnamon
  • and two tablespoons of melted butter

Steps

  1. warm milk in saucepan, remove from heat
  2. bloom yeast in warm water in a large bowl
  3. mix in raisins, eggs, a half cup of the sugar, and the margarine until it is combined.
  4. stir in cooled milk and add flour and salt gradually
  5. remove dough to a lightly floured surface
  6. kneed for a few minutes, transfer to greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth and allow dough to rise for an hour
  7. prepare three nine by five inch loaf pans
  8. deflate risen dough, move to floured surface, roll into large rectangle, about half an inch thick
  9. moisten top of dough with two tablespoons of milk
  10. combine remaining sugar with cinnamon
  11. sprinkle over moistened dough
  12. roll up tightly into a log
  13. cut large dough roll into thirds tuck under ends to form loaves
  14. place into prepared loaf pans and lightly grease tops
  15. cover and allow to rise once more, about an hour
  16. preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  17. bake until lightly browned and sounds hollow when tapped, about 45 minutes
  18. remove loaves and brush with melted butter
